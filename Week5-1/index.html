Pablo Picasso sketches range in subject matter and style but retain the energy
and elegance that prove that everything can be depicted with only few lines.

Owl

Picasso's range in line at mostly dealt with animals and refled his enduring love for animals.
They stand out for being so simple, yet they show a mastery of a craft due to how expertly
Picasso is able to depict an animal in so few lines. In this Owl sketch,
Picasso is able to portray the bird in only nine lines.

Dog

Even here, we are able to see Picasso's skill, potraying a dog in only one line. Although known
for his work and influence in cubism and surrealism, it is these sketches
that seem to have the most impact to my creativity. His sketches
are simple, yet elegant; exactly how'd you prefer a Website to be.

Face-Dove

Although a bit more complicated, Face-Dove combines human and animal qualities into one, meshing each
other into one using the very lines that are creating each object (the dove's wing also
being the side of the face).
